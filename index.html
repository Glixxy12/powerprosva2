About Us

Founded in 2019 and proudly serving Harrisonburg, VA, Power Pros VA delivers expert exterior cleaning services for both residential and commercial properties. From pressure washing to window and gutter cleaning, we’re committed to restoring and maintaining the beauty of your property. Available 7 AM – 5 PM, our team brings professionalism and precision to every job.

Our Services

Pressure Washing

Remove tough grime, dirt, and algae from driveways, sidewalks, decks, and more. Our pressure washing services restore the look and safety of your surfaces.

Window Cleaning

Crystal-clear, streak-free windows that let the light shine in. We clean both interior and exterior glass for a polished look every time.

Gutter Cleaning

Prevent water damage with our thorough gutter cleaning service. We remove debris to keep your gutters flowing and your foundation protected.

Exterior Cleaning

From siding to fences, we wash away dirt, mildew, and buildup to refresh your home’s entire exterior.
